About Us

Welcome to the world of Beasties – a captivating NFT collection of 10,000 unique pieces. Each Beastie represents an animal rescued by PFA Bangalore, a devoted organization committed to safeguarding and nurturing our precious wildlife. By owning a Beastie, you not only acquire a one-of-a-kind digital masterpiece but also contribute to a greater cause. 30% of the profits generated from this collection will directly fund PFA's ongoing efforts to protect and care for animals in need. Join us in celebrating the resilience of these magnificent creatures and making a meaningful impact on their lives. Together, we can create a brighter future for our furry friends.

What is a beasty?

The Beasties are 2-D art pieces generated programmatically using a python based program. All the traits have been created by a 16 year old animal lover-Simrat Oberoi. The different characters represent the animals that PFA Banglore has helped save in their locality. There are a total of 10000 pieces.

What does it mean to own a beasty?

As you mint and purchase your Beasty, we ensure that you acquire the intellectual property rights for each individual token. This empowers you with a unique level of ownership and creative control. The first 500 buyers will recieve an exclusive nft of an unreleased character ;) All the buyers will have access to an exclusive discord server.
